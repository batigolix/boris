About

I'm Boris Doesborg (Venlo (NL), 1971) and I am a Drupal web developer, analyst and documentation writer based in Utrecht.

Currently I am working as Drupal developer at Finalist in Rotterdam & Maarssen.

I have a masters degree in Business Communication (Universiteit Utrecht, Netherlands) and started my career authoring on line help systems and web sites.

After using many different content managment systems over several years I discovered Drupal (4.something). From then on I specialised in Drupal and worked as webmaster, developer, technical writer and project manager in various Drupal projects in companies and organisations in Spain, Belgium and the Netherlands.

I've been involved in the Drupal community as documentation writer, event organizer and volunteer. Currently I write and maintain documentation such as the Drupal 8 user guide.

I contribute to Drupal by organizing events, writing documentation, translating and helping out other users in forums, issue queues and Stack exchange.
