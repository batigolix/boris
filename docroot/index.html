About

I'm Boris Doesborg (Venlo (NL), 1971) and I work as developer, analyst and technical writer specialised in Drupal websites.

I have a masters degree in Business Communication (Universiteit Utrecht, Netherlands) and started working in the late nineties writing on line help systems and internet pages.

A years long search for a decent content management system to support such publications led me to Drupal (4.something). Over the last years I worked as webmaster, developer, technical writer and project manager in various Drupal projects in companies and organisations in Spain and Belgium.

I've been involved in Drupal as member of the documentation team (writing among others the embedded Drupal 7 help (http://www.example.com/admin/help), by helping out in forums, translating, being a driving force behind the Dutch speaking Drupal website and by organizing events (most notably the Fosdem devrooms and the Drupal Developer Days Brussels 2011).

I contribute to Drupal by organizing events, writing documentation, translating and helping out other users in forums and issue queues.
