About

I'm Boris Doesborg (Venlo (NL), 1971) and I work as developer, analyst and documentation writer specialised in Drupal websites.

Currently I am working as a freelance Drupal developer at Wunderkraut Nederland in Utrecht.

I have a masters degree in Business Communication (Universiteit Utrecht, Netherlands) and started working in the late nineties writing on line help systems and internet pages.

A years long search for a decent content management system to support such publications led me to Drupal (4.something). Over the last years I worked as webmaster, developer, technical writer and project manager in various Drupal projects in companies and organisations in Spain, Belgium and the Netherlands.

I've been involved in the Drupal community as documentation writer, event organizer and volunteer. Currently I am member of the Drupal documentation working groups) and I write and maintain the Drupal 8 user guide.

I contribute to Drupal by organizing events, writing documentation, translating and helping out other users in forums, issue queues and Stack exchange.
